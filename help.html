Patient Selection

1. Select a Patient

  • You can select a patient, by clicking on the row of the patient to be treated in the table.
    The name of the selected patient is now displayed above the table.
  • Searching:
    You can search lexicographical for patients via the searchbar.

2. Add a new Patient

  • You can add new patients, by clicking on the "plus-button".
    Afterwards it will show you a popup-window.
  • Please enter the firstname, lastname and date of birth of the new patient.
  • Click on "add".
  • After clicking on the "refresh-button", the new patient will appear in the table.

3. Edit a Patient

  • Once you have selected a patient, you can edit the patient by clicking on the "pencil-button".
    Afterwards it will show you a popup-window.
  • Now you can change the firstname, lastname and the date of birth of the patient.
  • Click on "edit".
  • After clicking on the "refresh-button", the changes of the patient will appear in the table.

4. Delete a Patient

  • You can delete a patient, by clicking on the "minus-button".
    After a Confirmation with "OK", the patient will be deleted of the table.
